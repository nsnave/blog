My First Publication!

Post 6 | February 26, 2023

I'm proud to annouce that I have gotten my first peer-reviewed publication! It is to be included in ACM's ICSE conference proceedings.

For the last couple of years, I have been working on research on programming languages for quantum computers. This work focussed on the implementation and design of a programming language based on measurement-based quantum computation (MBQC) as opposed to the standard gate-based models of quantum computation. This past summer I presented the research at the conference Quantum Physics and Logic (QPL) 2022 held at the University of Oxford. It was so much fun and such a great experience, both personally and professionaly. Here is the presentation and here is the arXiv preprint from then!

I'm now proud to annouce that this work has been accepted to ACM's 45th International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE) as a part of their Quantum Software Engineering workshop (Q-SE). Not only will I be presenting it there but it will also be published (!) in the ACM's proceedings of the conference. :-D

-- Aidan
